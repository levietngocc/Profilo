⟡ SELF-LEARNING PROJECT / INTERNSHIP

REPAIR MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Power Apps · Power Automate · Power BI · Digital Transformation

🎯 Role & Objective

Role: Full-stack Developer (Self-learning Project)
Goal: Digitize the repair management workflow for traditional electronics shops – replacing paper records with a structured database system.

This project originated from real experience working at a hardware repair shop. It manages the entire device lifecycle: intake, diagnosis, quotation, repair, and return.

⚙️ Skills gained

  • Power Apps – Built a canvas app for shop staff to create, update, and track repair tickets.
  • Power Automate – Designed approval flows for quotation and automated notifications. (Limited by edu email constraints – theoretical design implemented.)
  • Power BI – Created dashboards to visualize repair volume, technician performance, and revenue.
  • Database Design – Implemented relational schema in SSMS (SQL Server) with tables for inventory, repair tickets, and spare parts used.
  • AI Pair Programming – Used Gemini AI to troubleshoot Power Automate expressions and optimize workflows.

🗄️ Database Schema (Dataverse / SharePoint)

※ Implemented via SharePoint lists & Dataverse due to Power Platform constraints. Tables and columns as used in the actual system.
